# WorkWise Enterprise 🌟

> Your AI-powered office assistant — built to make work simpler, faster, and smarter.

---

## What is WorkWise?

WorkWise is a smart AI assistant designed for modern teams and professionals. Whether you need to draft a professional email, summarize a long report, create meeting minutes, or translate documents — WorkWise does it all in seconds.

No more staring at a blank page. Just tell WorkWise what you need, and it gets to work.

---

## Features ✨

- ✉️ **Draft Emails** — Professional emails written in seconds
- 📋 **Summarize** — Turn long documents into clear insights
- 📝 **Meeting Minutes** — Structured notes from your discussions
- 🔁 **Rewrite & Polish** — Elevate any content to boardroom quality
- 📊 **Business Reports** — Complete reports with analysis and recommendations
- ✅ **Action Items** — Extract tasks, owners, and deadlines instantly
- 🌍 **Translate** — 7 languages with professional tone
- 💼 **Write Proposals** — Compelling business proposals in minutes
- 📂 **Document Upload** — Analyze your own files
- 🕐 **Usage History** — Every conversation saved and searchable

---

## Tech Stack 🛠️

- **Frontend** — React.js
- **Authentication** — Firebase Auth (Email + Google)
- **Database** — Firebase Firestore
- **AI** — Google Gemini API
- **Hosting** — Vercel
